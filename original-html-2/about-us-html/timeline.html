Our Timeline

Our founders set out with a simple mission: to create a hotel that blends comfort, elegance, and heartfelt hospitality. Planning and architectural design began with a focus on timeless design and guest-centered spaces.

Discover the milestones and achievements that define our hotel's journey over the years.

2007

The hotel officially opened its doors with 80 luxury rooms, a signature restaurant, and a commitment to world-class service. Within the first year, it became a preferred stay for business travelers and tourists alike

2012

Due to growing demand, a new wing was added, increasing capacity to 140 rooms.

2018

Online booking systems were upgraded, mobile check-in was introduced, and smart concierge services were added to enhance convenience and personalization.

2020

All guest rooms were redesigned with contemporary interiors, smart room technology, and enhanced comfort features. Guest satisfaction ratings reached an all-time high.

2026 and present

With almost two decades of hospitality excellence, the hotel continues to innovate while staying true to its founding values: comfort, elegance, and genuine service.
